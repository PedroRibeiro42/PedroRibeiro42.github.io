Evaluation
  • Evaluation Formula
  • Weekly Problems
    • #00 - Introduction to the course and the online judges (not counting to grade)
    • #01 - Sublinear complexity data structures (map, set, priority_queue)
    • #02 - Sorting, binary search, ternary search, bisection
    • #03 - Sqrt decomposition, segment trees and variants
    • #04 - Cumulative sums and fenwick trees (BITs)
    • #05 - Dynamic Programming I (classic)
    • #06 - Dynamic Programming II (partitions, games, dags, counting, search)
    • #07 - Strings (KMP, Aho-Corasick, tries, suffix trees, suffix arrays)
    • #08 - Graphs I (DFS, BFS, topological sorting, articulation points, bridges, euler paths, ssc)
    • #09 - Graphs II (distances: dijkstra, bellman-ford, floyd; MST: prim+kruskall; max flow)
    • #10 - Computational Geometry (points, lines, polygons, intersections, areas, inclusion, convex hull)
  • Competitive Events
    • 1st Contest: outside class, 2h30m (problems similar to CodeForces Round) [early december?]
    • 2nd Contest: during class, 3h (partial points per problem) [12/01?]
    • 3rd Contest: January of February, 5h (final contest) [late Jan, early Feb?]
